# Contributing to MKRBOX

Thanks for helping build the Modular Kinematic Replicator.

## Ways to contribute
- Spec reviews and research notes.
- Tool/module concepts and CAD sketches.
- Simulation levels and failure scenarios.
- Documentation edits and copy improvements.

## Getting started
1. Read `SPEC.md`, `MODULES.md`, `LEVELS.md`, and `SIM.md`.
2. Open a GitHub issue with your proposal.
3. Share early drafts in the community channel once it is live.

## Review process (draft)
- Keep proposals scoped and testable.
- Provide a short summary, goals, and open questions.
- Expect iterative feedback from maintainers and other builders.
